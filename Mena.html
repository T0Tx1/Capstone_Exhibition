Project Statement

Embracing Surveillance is about taking control in a world where being watched feels inevitable. Instead of resisting the spotlight, this project flips the script—what happens when we choose to give them a show? Through surreal, almost dreamlike visuals, the work blends light and shadow, offering a reflection on perception and choice. It’s about asking, “If the world’s eyes are on you, how do you shape your story?”

Artist Statement

I create because I’m fascinated by the tension between who we are and how we’re seen. My work plays with themes like identity, technology, and connection—sometimes by mixing natural, organic visuals with AI-generated elements. It’s less about giving answers and more about starting conversations. Whether it’s a surreal scene or an abstract idea, I want to make people stop, question, and maybe even rethink how they see the world (and themselves).
