Project Statement

To match the theme of our exhibition, I return to the fragment of my life where everything is a constant decision that I need to make. Decision paralysis comes in a pair with anxiety, and it can paralyze you mentally and physically from making the actions you need to take. Sounds and visuals that evoke this sense of time running out have been compiled into one. Everyone goes through a similar journey and it is up to them to either freeze in time or to accept the fact that they need to make the next move.
